Bio

Hi! I am Pranav Harwadekar, a Computer Science Honors student at Texas A&M University. I am a passionate developer, researcher, and learner with experience spanning industry, research, and product development. I interned at McKesson (Supplylogix) as a Software Engineering Intern, where I built large-scale forecasting pipelines with PySpark and MLflow to optimize pharmacy inventory across 10,000+ sites. I previously completed data analytics externships with Beats by Dre—where I was selected as one of just 5 representatives nationwide to present directly to the Head of Consumer Insights—and Webacy Inc., a Web3 security startup focusing on smart contract risk assessment.

On campus, I lead the Autonomous Navigation team for Texas A&M's international rover project, building ROS2-based navigation and Stereo SLAM systems. My research experience includes undergraduate ML projects through the Aggie Research Program and hyperspectral imaging algorithm development in CUDA and C++. I've also developed and launched apps on the App Store.

Beyond technical work, I serve as a Cengage Student Ambassador, Engineering Honors Executive Committee Service Chair, and former UNICEF USA National Youth Ambassador, combining leadership and service with innovation. I am currently a Software Engineering Fellow at Headstarter AI, building AI-powered products with React, Next.js, and Vercel.
